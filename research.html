Research

The main theme of my research has always been in the area of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning. My research work is inherently multidisciplinary. Currently, I am focusing on the following research areas:

I am motivated to learn, work upon, and teach new technologies in the field of computer science. I am always delighted to supervise strongly motivated Master's and PhD students, who are willing to work with me in my research areas (broadly, Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning).
