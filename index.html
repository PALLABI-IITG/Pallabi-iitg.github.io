About Me

Dr. Pallabi Saikia received her Bachelor's Degree with an award of Gold Medal in Computer Science and Engineering from the North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology. Later, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in the domain of multi-sensors data for reservoir characterization, she obtained her PhD degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. Before joining in Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Jais, she has also served as an Assistant Professor in Pandit Deendayal Energy University. She has published several reputed journals, conferences papers, and book chapters. She received many conference travel grants, WCCI Travel Grant, SMC Travel Grant and GHCI Scholarship to present her research works across different countries. Her primary research interests include Deep Learning Applications, AI-based Generative models, Adversarial Machine Learning, Modelling on Multisensory and multimodal data. She is involved with professional bodies of Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), and IEEE Women in Engineering Society for conducting and collaborating research-related activities with different individuals from different organizations and regions.
